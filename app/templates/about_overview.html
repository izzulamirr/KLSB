Company Evolution

Kemuncak Lanai Sdn Bhd (KLSB), established in 2011, is a professional service provider with core expertise in Technical Manpower Supply and 3D Modelling & Design Consultancy. Over the years, we have progressively broadened our capabilities—now spanning Advanced Work Packaging (AWP), Specialized Inspection Services and Cybersecurity Consultancy. This evolution reflects disciplined capability building to deliver reliable, value‑driven outcomes.
