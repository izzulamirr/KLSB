Inspection Services

We provide specialized inspection solutions tailored for the oil & gas, petrochemical, and power plant industries. Through our strong collaboration with Agensi Nuklear Malaysia (ANM), we offer cutting-edge, non-invasive inspection methods designed to tackle complex industrial challenges.

Our advanced techniques ensure accurate diagnostics, improved plant reliability, and enhanced operational efficiency, helping industries maintain safety, performance, and compliance with minimal downtime.

Gamma Scanning

A non-invasive technique that evaluates process columns, reactors, and vessels without requiring shutdowns.

  • Internal blockages and malfunctions
  • Liquid maldistribution in packed/tray columns
  • Mechanical damage and efficiency issues

Neutron Backscatter Technique

Ideal for detecting Moisture Under Insulation (MUI) and other anomalies in pipelines and structures.

  • Identification of moisture intrusion without removing insulation
  • Preventive maintenance to reduce corrosion risks
  • Quick and cost-effective assessment

Industrial Radiotracer Technology

A powerful tool for diagnosing flow behavior and process efficiency.

  • Leak detection in pipelines and heat exchangers
  • Flow distribution analysis in complex systems
  • Residence time distribution (RTD) studies for process optimization
